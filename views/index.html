Problem

Bike sharing is not a new business. The 1st generation is public dock-based bike-share systems initiated by local authorities. The 2nd generation is GPS-based dockless systems which is prevalent in the world now. Despite the popularity of these models, it causes many problems

Centralisation

The earlier bike sharing models are heavily centralised. It is costly, inefficient and unscalable as fleet operators must replicate the whole value chain in every city.

Centralised systems are also prone to dangers of privacy violation, data misuse or theft.

Negative Social Impact

Social problems of theft, vandalism and abandonment are commonplace in dockless bike shares with a focus on one-way rides.

It also incurs huge business operating costs to haul bikes back, find abandoned bikes and fight theft.

Constraints on Evolution of Urban Mobility

Low price structure of earlier bike sharing systems limits the cycling equipment to inexpensive and durable bikes, thus, restricting technology choices and limiting the advance of urban mobility.

Our Solution

A blockchain protocol for a 3rd Generation Bike Sharing - 'BikeCoin Network'

Decentralised Bike Sharing System

BikeCoin decentralises the various components of bike sharing system and streamlines the cost and time required to launch ride sharing services. Multiple parties can now collaborate seamlessly to launch its own services and receive a fair revenue share any time and any where using smart contracts. As a result, it creates a fair competition and sustainable network effects.

Promote Responsible Ridership

With a current focus on premium smart bicycles, we believe that by providing better quality bicycles, flexible pricing and encouraging round-trip rides, BikeCoin can promote a sustainable urban transport and overcomes negative social impacts that plague earlier bike sharing schemes. In the future, we will extend our services to all type of bikes and IoT devices by providing a firmware library for any manufacturer or assembler to implement onto their products.

Highly Scalable Services Solution

Our protocol combines the strengths and avoids the weaknesses of Ethereum blockchain by having a side chain solution ‘BikeCoin chain’ using Proof-of-Stake (POS) consensus. It ensures low latencies, immediate transaction finality, high performance and excellent scalability to our network.

Data Privacy

Whereas certain information is recorded for transparency on blockchain, commercial and personal data will be end-to-end encrypted and “hidden” using our Zero-Knowledge Proof. Thus, users can take advantage of both privacy and decentralised security at the same time.

Open-Source Infrastructure for Mobility Decentralised Apps (DApps)

BikeCoin’s DApps currently supports decentralised bike registration, fleet operations and peer to peer sharing. Since we are an open-source protocol, DApps developers can leverage BikeCoin technologies and data to quickly deploy and scale their DApps on top of the BikeCoin Network for bike sharing services or any other shared mobility solutions.
