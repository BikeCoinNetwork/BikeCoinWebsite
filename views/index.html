About

VeloxChain is an open-source, scalable blockchain infrastructure designed to connect urban mobility.

It promotes decentralised collaboration in the transport and mobility sector by leveraging the power of sharing economy and blockchain technology. Thus, people and businesses worldwide can co-create, access and benefit from a global network of shared mobility services.

As a result, VeloxChain accelerates the world’s transition to an open, seamless and interconnected urban mobility.

Our Products

Open Protocol for Decentralised Mobility Apps (DApps)

With Proof-of-stake voting consensus, VeloxChain protocol achieves high transaction throughput with 1000 tx/s and 2-second block-time. The protocol also enables privacy-preserving collaboration which allows users to take advantage of transparency without compromising security and privacy for data sharing. Whereas certain information is recorded publicly on the blockchain, commercial and personal data will be end-to-end encrypted and “hidden” using our Zero-Knowledge Proof.

Velox Scan Velox Block State

Decentralised application (Dapp) development solution

VeloxDev makes it easy and hassle-free to develop, deploy and manage Dapps on top of the Velox Protocol for any other shared mobility services using our built-in components, SDK and APIs.

Decentralised mobility marketplace for business

An open marketplace platform with bundled features for mobility operators, vehicle providers, retailer service providers and other third-party entities to connect, collaborate and co-create services.

VeloxBiz decentralises the various components of the shared-mobility system and streamlines the cost and time required to start up a business. Multiple merchants can now collaborate seamlessly to launch their services and receive a fair revenue share anytime and anywhere using smart contracts.

VeloxBiz

Decentralised Mobility as a Service for users

VeloxGo is a DApp to connect users and mobility providers, allowing them access to a variety of transport modes to meet their mobility needs under a unified account and payment. Velox ecosystem intends to start with bike sharing and micro-mobility like e-scooters or segways before venturing into the sharing of cars and other vehicles.

Moreover, mobility entrepreneurs can customise VeloxGo to build their sharing-mobility DApps.

Roadmap

Q4-2017 to Q1-2018: Concept Design
  • Idea conception and development
  • Validate technical feasibility: zero-knowledge proof and full-homomorphic encryption module
  • Team and Partner onboarded
Q2-2018: Proof of Concept
  • Develop market research, business models
  • Vehicle ownership (ERC721) protocol
  • Meta Transaction Relay Protocol
  • Micro-payment channel smart contract framework
  • IPFS p2p storage layers
Q3-2018: Product Development
  • Design VeloxBiz : the marketplace for sharing-mobility providers, vehicle suppliers
  • VeloxChain block explorer
  • VeloxGo: an e-mobility wallet
  • Implement a dual-token Proof Of Stake Consensus
Q4-2018: Dual token PoS test-net
  • Launch VeloxChain test-net with over 1000 tx/s.
  • VeloxBiz: “Alibaba” marketplace for sharing-mobility providers.
  • Forming strategic partnership with business and technology partners.
Q2-2019: Main-net
  • VeloxChain main-net with Dual-token PoS
  • VeloxBiz: fleet management system for service operators, network operators for bike-sharing, scooter-sharing and segway-sharing services.
  • Velox Dev tools and packages for sharing-mobility DApps.
Q2-2019: Opening Ecosystem
  • VeloxGo - MaaS mobility wallet.
  • VeloxDev tools for sharing-mobility DApps such as charging sytem
Q1-2020: Interoperability ecosystem
  • Cross-chain with other protocols such as Ethereum
  • Develop Sharding solution
  • Develop PoS Hybrid solution
